The Andes Mountains are the longest continental mountain range in the world, forming a continuous highland along the western edge of South America. The range is 6,999 km long, 200 to 700 km wide and has an average height of about 4,000 m . The Andes extend from north to south through seven South American countries: Venezuela, Colombia, Ecuador, Peru, Bolivia, Chile, and Argentina
